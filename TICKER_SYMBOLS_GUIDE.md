## üö® Troubleshooting

### If Ticker Doesn't Work:
1. **Check spelling** - Must be exact (e.g., `AAPL` not `Apple`)
2. **Use uppercase** - `AAPL` not `aapl`
3. **Verify listing** - Must be on NYSE or NASDAQ
4. **Check trading hours** - Some data may be delayed
5. **Try alternative** - If one fails, try another from the list

### Common Issues:
- **"Insufficient price history"** - Ticker too new or delisted
- **"Invalid ticker symbol"** - Spelling error or doesn't exist
- **"Timeout"** - Backend issue, retry
